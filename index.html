Joshua Neronha

Airline Strategist, Data Scientist, and Engineer

I enjoy solving unique problems with data, particularly those related to aviation.

Examples include working in an optics laboratory as an undergraduate researcher optimizing antenna designs and researching how airlines plan their networks. Additionally, I have a strong interest in fluid mechanics. I also love traveling and spending time outdoors, particularly running and backpacking. I'm very excited to be working at American Airlines as a Strategy and Planning Analyst after graduating in May 2022!
